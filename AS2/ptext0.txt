Thomas Connery was born at the Royal Maternity Hospital in Edinburgh, Scotland on 25 August 1930; he was named after his paternal grandfather. He was brought up at No. 176 Fountainbridge, a block which has since been demolished. His mother, Euphemia McBain "Effie" McLean, was a cleaning woman.
